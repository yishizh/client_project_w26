How to initiate

Initiating networking conversations can be intimidating, but with the right approach, it can lead to meaningful connections. Start by researching the person or organization you want to connect with. Prepare thoughtful questions and topics to discuss. Be genuine and show interest in their work. Remember, networking is about building relationships, not just asking for favors.

Here are some conversation starters: 1. "I noticed you work in [industry/role]. Can you tell me more about your experience?" 2. "What advice would you give to someone looking to enter this field?" 3. "Are there any upcoming events or groups you recommend for networking in this area?" 4. "How did you get started in your career?" 5. "What skills do you think are most important for success in this industry?"
